About me

My name is Bill Barekas, I am an experienced software engineer. I live in Patras, Greece. This site is my little space on web to write anything I want to write. I use Github Pages to host this and Jekyll to edit my blog. I have big plans for this site but not much time to do them. My little son get all my free time when I am at home.

One of the things I want to do is to maintain a personal blog, writing about my activities mainly the non-computerized ones. I like to share experiences from things I do, places I visit and some usual useless information that might be interesting for someone.
